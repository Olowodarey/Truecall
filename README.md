# ⚽₿ TrueCall

> The Fantasy League for Bitcoin Traders

TrueCall is an on-chain prediction platform on **Stacks** where you compete by making Bitcoin market calls, earning points, and climbing leaderboards. Think Fantasy Premier League, but for BTC price action, dominance, ETF flows, and on-chain metrics.

---

## How It Works

1. Connect your wallet
2. Join a public season or private league
3. Stake STX or sBTC
4. Submit Bitcoin predictions
5. Earn points for accuracy, streaks, and consistency
6. Top-ranked players share the reward pool at season end
7. Unlock soulbound badges as proof of skill

---

## Key Features

- **Bitcoin-only predictions** — price, dominance, ETF flows, on-chain signals
- **Seasonal competition** — recurring cycles with leaderboard-based payouts
- **Private leagues** — invite-only for communities, DAOs, and trading groups
- **On-chain reputation** — soulbound badges tied to your wallet
- **Oracle resolution** — outcomes settled via Pyth Network
