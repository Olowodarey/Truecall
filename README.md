

# ⚽₿ TrueCall

### The Fantasy League for Bitcoin Traders

**TrueCall** is a gamified, on-chain prediction platform on **Stacks** where Bitcoin traders, analysts, and enthusiasts compete by making BTC market calls, earning points, climbing leaderboards, and building a verifiable reputation.

Think **Fantasy Premier League**, but instead of picking football players, you predict **Bitcoin price action, dominance, ETF flows, and on-chain metrics**.

Players join public seasons or private leagues, lock in predictions with **STX** or **sBTC**, and earn rewards for accuracy, streaks, and leaderboard performance. The best performers unlock **soulbound badges** and establish an on-chain track record that cannot be faked, edited, or deleted.

At the end of each competition, the **top winners share the reward pool based on final leaderboard position**, turning market insight into real on-chain winnings.

**No KYC. No gatekeepers. Just conviction, competition, and on-chain bragging rights.**

## How It Works

### 1. Join a Season

Users enter a public season or private league.

Each season runs as a competitive cycle where players answer a stream of Bitcoin-related questions.

### 2. Make Bitcoin Calls

Players respond to prediction prompts such as:

* Will BTC close above a given price this week?
* Will BTC dominance rise above a threshold?
* Will ETF inflows exceed a target?
* Will a specific on-chain metric turn bullish?

### 3. Stake Conviction

Each player stakes **STX** or **sBTC** when submitting predictions.

These entries collectively build the competition pool.

### 4. Earn Points

Users earn points based on:

* correct predictions
* early correct entries
* consecutive streaks
* long-term consistency

### 5. Climb the Leaderboard

Players compete for placement in:

* **Seasonal leaderboards**
* **Private league tables**
* **All-time rankings**

### 6. Share the Reward Pool

When the season or event ends, the **top-ranked players receive the pooled rewards** according to a predefined distribution model.

For example, the final reward pool can be shared among top finishers such as:

* **1st place — 30%**
* **2nd place — 25%**
* **3rd place — 20%**
* **4th place — 15%**
* **5th place — 8%**

The remaining **2%** can be reserved as a protocol or platform fee.

This creates a strong competitive incentive: the better your performance, the larger your share of the pool.

### 7. Unlock Badges and Reputation

Top performers also earn **soulbound NFT badges** that prove their forecasting skill on-chain.
